Key responsibilities

  • Manage event marketing planning process, timelines, production flow; help establish event marketing operating procedures and plans
  • Collaborate with a wide variety of internal stakeholders and external agencies, sponsors and vendors to drive the development of assets to support these plans
  • Ensure that all deliverables meet corporate brand guidelines as well as quality standards
  • Manage event program budgets and vendor relationships
  • Collaborate with others in marketing, sales and customer success to identify and address areas for event improvement in our strategy, processes and execution
  • Develop event presentations and other documents to clearly communicate strategic plans to management, cross-functional partners and to the broader organization
  • Drive alignment and partner closely with cross-functional teams to identify opportunities and projects to elevate through events
  • Design, develop and maintain process workflows to support various event marketing programs
